The Man Who Made Me

Expectations

October 12th 2018 is a day that will forever live in my memory. That day my father suddenly passed away. Jeffrey Wayne Evans wore many hats in his life from his days as a Volunteer Firefighter becoming the Lieutenant of Station 8 to creating and running his own Small Business (Jeffery's Attic) while still working a full time job. His work ethic was unquestionable and my father left huge shoes to fill. This started my path in writing as an outlet to understand and come to terms with our loss. My poem Expectations was written for my father and can be read here via Wattpad.
